SafeGoals

A football intelligence platform designed to help users understand goal risk before making decisions. SafeGoals focuses on probability, context, and discipline — not hype.

What is SafeGoals?

SafeGoals analyzes football matches using Goal Scoring Probability (GSP) to classify games into Low, Medium, or High Risk for common goal markets.

Instead of telling you what to bet on, SafeGoals helps you avoid bad decisions by highlighting risk levels clearly and transparently.

How SafeGoals Helps

Risk-First Analysis

Every match is evaluated based on historical goal tendencies, not assumptions or hype.

Over 0.5 & Over 1.5 Focus

Clear risk breakdown for each team scoring and combined goal outcomes.

Transparency

No “sure games”, no fake accuracy claims. Just probabilities and structured analysis.

Decision Discipline

SafeGoals is built to help users pass risky games, not chase every fixture.
